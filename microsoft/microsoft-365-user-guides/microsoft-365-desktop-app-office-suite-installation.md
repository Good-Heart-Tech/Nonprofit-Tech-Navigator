# Microsoft 365 Desktop App (Office Suite) Installation

## :checkered\_flag: Prerequisites

To download the Microsoft 365 Apps (Office suite), you must have a Microsoft 365 Business Standard or above license. Please see [this section](https://learn.goodhearttech.org/microsoft-365/microsoft-365-setup-for-nonprofits) for more info about licensing available to nonprofits.&#x20;

## :technologist: Logging in to Microsoft 365

After verifying you're properly licensed, you're ready to download the Microsoft Apps (formerly Office Apps).\
<br>

* Navigate to this URL: [https://m365.cloud.microsoft/apps/](https://m365.cloud.microsoft/apps/)&#x20;
* Sign in with your Microsoft 365 organization account.&#x20;
* It should prompt you for a sign-in or bring you to this page:

<div align="left"><figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (57).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

* Next, we can click on the "**Install Apps**" dropdown:

<div align="left"><figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (56).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

Select "**Microsoft 365 Apps**" and it should begin the download:\
![](<../../.gitbook/assets/image (23).png>)

* Once downloaded, you can find the installer in your **Downloads** folder, or press "Ctrl+J" to open your browsers downloads window.&#x20;
* Click on the "_OfficeSetup.exe_" file. It may prompt you to "_Allow this App to make changes to your device_", click **yes**. If it prompts for a username and password, ask your administrator.&#x20;

You should see the following window while it installs - it may take a bit:

![](<../../.gitbook/assets/image (24).png>)

Once its done, you should see this window:\
![](<../../.gitbook/assets/image (25).png>)

Well done! You can start using your apps by searching for the newly installed apps in your start menu.

If you get an error or have issues, reach out to us at [support@goodhearttech.org](mailto:support@goodhearttech.org).&#x20;
