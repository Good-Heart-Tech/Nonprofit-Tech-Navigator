# How to Update Your Microsoft 365 Password

1. Go to [https://myaccount.microsoft.com](https://myaccount.microsoft.com).
2. Sign in with your email address and current password.
3. Click **Change Password**.
4. Enter your old password once.
5. Enter your new password twice (to confirm).
6. Click **Submit**.

Your password is now updated. Don’t forget to update it on your phone, tablet, or Outlook app so you don’t get sign-in errors.
