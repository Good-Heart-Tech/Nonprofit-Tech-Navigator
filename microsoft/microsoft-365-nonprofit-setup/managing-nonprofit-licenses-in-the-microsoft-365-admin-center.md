# Managing Nonprofit Licenses in the Microsoft 365 Admin Center

{% hint style="info" %}
This guide walks you through **purchasing paid** nonprofit licenses in the Microsoft 365 Admin Center for your organization. If you are looking for the **FREE** licenses, [please go here](../../google-workspace/google-workspace-nonprofit-setup/configure-google-workspace-for-nonprofit.md).
{% endhint %}

## :checkered\_flag: Prerequisites

**Once your nonprofit has been approved for Microsoft 365 nonprofit licensing, you can continue through this process.** You can verify your organization's nonprofit status with Microsoft [using this guide.](how-to-apply-for-free-nonprofit-microsoft-365-services.md)

Before purchasing licenses, ensure:

* You are assigned the **Global Admin** or **Billing Admin** role
* You have access to the Microsoft 365 Admin Center
* A valid payment method is available

If your organization has not yet been verified, visit the Microsoft Nonprofit Program to begin the eligibility verification process. Please see this page:

## Manage Microsoft Licenses in the Microsoft 365 Admin Center

1. Sign in to [https://admin.microsoft.com](https://admin.microsoft.com/) with your Global Admin or Billing Admin account.
2. In the left-hand navigation menu, select **Billing**, then click **Licenses**

#### View available licenses (what you own vs. what’s assigned)

1. Go to **Billing** → **Licenses**.
2. Select a product to see **Total**, **Assigned**, and **Available** counts.

#### Assign or unassign licenses

1. Go to **Users** → **Active users**.
2. Select users (checkboxes). Select **Manage product licenses**.
3. Choose the license, then select **Assign** or **Remove**. Select **Save**.

{% hint style="warning" %}
Unassigning a license can remove access to email and apps. Plan this before offboarding.
{% endhint %}

## Purchasing Licenses in the Microsoft 365 Admin Center

### Step 2: Navigate to Billing

1. Sign in to [https://admin.microsoft.com](https://admin.microsoft.com/) with your Global Admin or Billing Admin account.
2. In the left-hand navigation menu, select **Billing**, then click **Purchase services**

This section displays all available subscriptions your nonprofit is eligible to purchase.

### Step 3: Filter for Nonprofit Offers

1. Use the search bar or available filters within **Purchase services**.
2. Look for a tab on the screen labeled "**Nonprofit"**

#### If You Do Not See Nonprofit Licensing, or a "Nonprofit" tab

If nonprofit offers are not appearing:

* Verify that your organization has approved nonprofit status.
* Confirm you are signed into the correct tenant.
* Use the **Change billing account** button at the top of the scree&#x6E;**.**

You must select the billing account with:

**Agreement Type:** _Microsoft Online Subscription Agreement (MOSA)_

If multiple billing accounts exist, choose the one labeled **Microsoft Online Subscription Agreement (MOSA)** to access nonprofit subscription pricing.

### Step 4: Select the License

1. Click on the subscription you want to purchase
2. Review pricing (donated plans may show $0.00)
3. Click **Buy**

#### Common Nonprofit Subscriptions

* [Microsoft 365 Business Premium (Nonprofit Staff Pricing)](https://admin.cloud.microsoft/?#/catalog/offer-details/microsoft-365-business-premium-nonprofit-staff-pricing-/076D0DAD-8048-49DD-B13F-CF1D9BD06111)
* [Microsoft 365 Business Standard (Nonprofit Staff Pricing)](https://admin.cloud.microsoft/?#/catalog/offer-details/microsoft-365-business-standard-nonprofit-staff-pricing-/C6BB4EC6-07A6-419C-884E-88DA1479C4C8)
* [Microsoft 365 E3 (Nonprofit Staff Pricing)](https://admin.cloud.microsoft/?#/catalog/offer-details/microsoft-365-e3-nonprofit-staff-pricing-/BA3A4A76-FE86-4825-B640-F4435A0C6371)
* [Microsoft 365 E5 (Nonprofit Staff Pricing)](https://admin.cloud.microsoft/?#/catalog/offer-details/microsoft-365-e5-nonprofit-staff-pricing-/245A7344-0A36-4EDA-A0CF-F0FE92A5A210)

> **Note:** Available plans depend on your nonprofit eligibility status and tenant configuration.

### Step 5: Choose License Quantity

1. Enter the number of licenses needed
2. Select billing frequency (monthly or annual commitment, if applicable)
3. Click **Checkout**

> Tip: Start with the number of active users and adjust later if needed.

### Step 6: Complete Purchase

1. Confirm billing information
2. Review order details
3. Click **Place order**

Once completed, the licenses will be available in your tenant.

### Step 7: Assign Licenses to Users

After purchase:

1. Navigate to **Users > Active users**
2. Select a user
3. Click **Licenses and apps**
4. Toggle the new license to **On**
5. Click **Save changes**

Repeat for each user as needed.

### How to View or Manage Existing Subscriptions

To manage licenses:

1. Go to **Billing > Your products**
2. Select the subscription
3. You can:
   * Add more licenses
   * Reduce license count (at renewal or per terms)
   * Cancel subscription
   * View renewal date
