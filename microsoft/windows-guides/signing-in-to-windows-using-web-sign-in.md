# Signing In to Windows Using Web Sign-In

## How to Sign In

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (3).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Step 1:** At login, click **Sign-in options** (bottom-right).

**Step 2:** Click the **globe icon**.

**Step 3:** Enter your email and password in the browser that opens.

**Step 4:** Approve on your phone, use your fingerprint/face, or enter a code.



## Not Showing?

Contact your IT department; they may need to enable it for your account.
