# Signing In to Windows Using Web Sign-In

Sign into your computer using a browser—like signing into a website. More secure than passwords.

## How to Sign In

**Step 1:** At login, click **Sign-in options** (bottom-right).

![](.gitbook/assets/image%20%2860%29.png)

**Step 2:** Click the **globe icon**.

![](.gitbook/assets/image%20%2861%29.png)

**Step 3:** Enter your email and password in the browser that opens.

![](.gitbook/assets/image%20%2862%29.png)

**Step 4:** Approve on your phone, use your fingerprint/face, or enter a code.

![](.gitbook/assets/image%20%2863%29.png)

Done!

## Not Showing?

Contact IT—they may need to enable it for your account.

## Stuck?

| Problem | Try This |
|---------|----------|
| Browser won't open | Check internet, restart computer |
| Can't sign in | Verify email is correct, Caps Lock is off |
| Approval screen stuck | Wait 30 seconds, check your phone |
| Desktop won't load | Wait 60 seconds |

## Security

- Never share your password
- Lock your computer when you leave (Windows key + L)
- Tell IT if something looks wrong

## Questions?

Contact your IT department.
