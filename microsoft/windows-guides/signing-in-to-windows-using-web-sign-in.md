# Signing In to Windows Using Web Sign-In

## What is Web Sign-In?

Web Sign-In lets you sign into your computer by clicking a button instead of typing your password. Your browser opens automatically and handles the login—just like signing into a website.

**Why use it?**
- More secure than typing passwords
- Supports modern authentication (fingerprint, phone approval, etc.)
- Works great with Microsoft 365 accounts

{% hint style="info" %}
Web Sign-In only works on Windows 11. If you're using Windows 10, see [How to Check if You're Running Windows 10 or 11](how-to-check-if-youre-running-windows-10-or-11.md).
{% endhint %}

## How to Use Web Sign-In

### Step 1: Select Web Sign-In at Login

At the Windows login screen, click **Sign-in options** in the bottom-right corner.

![](.gitbook/assets/image%20%2860%29.png)

Select **Web sign-in** (looks like a globe icon).

![](.gitbook/assets/image%20%2861%29.png)

### Step 2: Sign In With Your Browser

A browser window opens automatically. Enter your email and password (usually your Microsoft 365 email).

![](.gitbook/assets/image%20%2862%29.png)

### Step 3: Approve Your Sign-In (if needed)

You might see one of these:

- **Approve on your phone** – Open your Authenticator app and approve the request
- **Use Windows Hello** – Use your fingerprint or face
- **Enter a code** – Type a code from your authenticator app

Just follow the prompts. It takes 30 seconds.

![](.gitbook/assets/image%20%2863%29.png)

### Step 4: You're In!

The browser closes automatically and you're signed into your computer.

## What If Web Sign-In Isn't Showing?

**Your organization disabled it.** Contact your IT department and ask them to enable Web Sign-In for your account.

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Browser won't open | Check your internet. Restart your computer and try again. |
| "Authentication failed" error | Make sure you're using the correct email. Check that Caps Lock isn't on. |
| Stuck on approval screen | Wait 30 seconds. Check your phone for an approval request. Try clicking "Sign in a different way." |
| Signed in but desktop won't load | Wait 30-60 seconds for your profile to load. This is normal. |

## Keep Your Account Safe

- **Never share your password** with anyone
- **Lock your computer** (Windows key + L) when you step away
- **Report suspicious activity** to your IT team immediately
