# Form Builders & e-Signature Solutions for Nonprofits

## eSignature platforms

eSignature platforms offer numerous advantages for nonprofits, including increased efficiency, reduced costs, and enhanced security. By eliminating the need for physical signatures, these platforms streamline workflows, save time, and reduce paper consumption. Some eSignature platforms offer free or discounted plans specifically designed for nonprofits.

### BoldSign

<div align="left"><figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/boldsign.svg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

[BoldSign ](https://boldsign.com/solutions/electronic-signature-for-non-profit-organizations)is a feature-rich and user-friendly eSignature platform that offers a comprehensive set of tools for document signing. **BoldSign is completely free for nonprofits, and we strongly recommend this solution**. [After creating a trial account](https://account.boldsign.com/signup?planId=101), simply send proof of nonprofit status to [sales@boldsign.com](mailto:sales@boldsign.com) to be upgraded to the _Growth_ plan for free. BoldSign integrates with Google, Microsoft, and others, simplifying workflows.

The free, nonprofit version of BoldSign is the equivalent of [the Growth plan](https://boldsign.com/electronic-signature-pricing/?plan=webapp), which includes:

* 3 users
* 150 signature requests/month
* 10 templates
* Custom branding

### JotForm Sign

<div align="left"><figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/JotForm.svg" alt="" width="150"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

The [JotForm Sign](https://www.jotform.com/products/sign/) free plan offers basic eSignature features like document signing and storage, but it has limitations on the number of documents you can sign per month (10) and the features available. While it's suitable for occasional use, organizations with higher volume needs may require a paid plan. [For nonprofits, JotForm offers discounted rates ](https://www.jotform.com/nonprofit/pricing/)for their paid plans.

### Microsoft SharePoint eSignature

[Microsoft SharePoint's eSignature solution](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365/syntex/esignature-send-requests) provides a secure method for obtaining digital signatures. It is free for nonprofits, though it requires activating the free nonprofit Azure subscription and [involves some administrative setup](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365/syntex/esignature-setup). Users need to manually format documents prior to sending them. Additionally, it offers a limited range of fields that can be requested, only including _Signature_, _Initials_, and _Date_.

## Form Builders

### Tally

[Tally](https://tally.so/) is a modern, no-code form builder ideal for nonprofits needing flexible data collection. It supports consent checkboxes and free-text fields for informal approvals.

**Free Plan Includes:**

* Unlimited forms and responses
* Conditional logic, calculations, hidden fields
* Stripe payments (with fees)
* Email notifications
* Embedding, redirect on submit, and custom "Thank You" pages
* Notion, Zapier, and Make integrations
* 1 workspace with collaborator access

Best for lightweight agreements, intake forms, and general data collection.

### Google Forms

[Google Forms ](https://forms.google.com)is included with Google Workspace for Nonprofits. It’s a simple, effective tool for surveys, registrations, and consent collection (non-legal).

**Features:**

* Unlimited forms/responses
* Required fields, checkboxes, and branching logic
* Google Sheets integration
* Embedding and sharing options
* File uploads (Workspace accounts only)
* Basic customization and collaboration

Ideal for basic workflows and internal data gathering.

### forms.app

<div align="left"><figure><img src="https://forms.app/cdn/icons/all/brands/formsapp-logo-dark.svg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

[forms.app](https://forms.app/) is a simple option for nonprofits that need forms, registrations, and basic approvals. The free tier is free forever.

**Free plan includes:**

* 5 forms
* 100 responses per month
* Unlimited fields per form
* 1 user

[Nonprofits and schools can apply for a 50% discount](https://profile.forms.app/education-npo-application) on paid plans. See [current pricing here](https://forms.app/en/pricing).

### Microsoft Forms

[Microsoft Forms](https://forms.office.com) is bundled with Microsoft 365 for Nonprofits. It supports surveys, polls, and simple approvals, but lacks legal eSignature support.

**Features:**

* Unlimited forms/responses (within tenant limits)
* Question branching, required fields, and consent checkboxes
* Excel and Power Automate integration
* Basic analytics and sharing controls
* Branding and collaboration via Microsoft 365

Great for organizations already using Microsoft tools.
