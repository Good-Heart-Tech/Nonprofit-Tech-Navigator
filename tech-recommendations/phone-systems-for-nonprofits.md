# 📞 Phone Systems for Nonprofits

{% hint style="success" %}
**What are softphones?**\
Softphones include all software-based methods of using your phone system, such as mobile apps (iOS/Android) or apps on your computer. They’re a great way to avoid the cost of physical desk phones.
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="warning" %}
Every nonprofit’s needs are different. We recommend running a trial or demo with each vendor before committing to a long-term plan.
{% endhint %}

## Microsoft Teams Voice Administration

### Teams Voice Administration

Microsoft Teams Voice can be a powerful phone system for nonprofits already invested in Microsoft 365. It works across devices (desktop, mobile, and desk phones) and supports both simple call setups and advanced features like call routing, auto attendants, and queuing.

Nonprofits also benefit from **discounted licensing** that can be purchased directly in the Microsoft Admin Portal catalog:

* **Teams Phone with Calling Plan (US)** – $11.20/user/month, includes phone numbers
* **Microsoft Teams Phone Resource Account** – 10 free
* **Microsoft Teams Rooms Basic** – 25 free
* **Microsoft Teams Rooms Basic without Audio Conferencing** – 25 free
* **Microsoft Teams Audio Conferencing with dial-out to USA/CAN** – Unlimited free

**Limitations to consider:**

* Teams Voice does not include SMS (texting) by default. You’ll need to purchase and integrate a third-party service if texting is important for your organization.
* Administration can be complex. Setting up call flows, resource accounts, and integrations requires technical knowledge. We strongly recommend working with a Managed Service Provider (MSP) or having a dedicated IT resource before committing to Teams Voice.

For Microsoft-focused organizations with IT support in place, Teams Voice offers deep integration and flexibility at a nonprofit-friendly price. For smaller nonprofits without IT capacity, a simpler provider (like Grasshopper or net2phone) is often a better fit.

## Paging and On-Call Management

### **OnPage**

<div align="left"><figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/onpage-logo.png.webp" alt="" width="246"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

[**OnPage** ](https://www.onpage.com/)is a full-featured, secure incident alerting and communication platform designed to support time-sensitive operations. It combines encrypted two-way messaging, escalation policies, on-call scheduling, voice capabilities, and audit trails into one mobile-friendly application. OnPage is particularly valuable for nonprofits needing reliable, urgent communications—whether for IT alerts, medical coordination, or crisis response.

Nonprofits benefit from OnPage’s advanced reliability and flexibility, including persistent alerts that override silent mode, automated escalations, and detailed delivery confirmation.

**OnPage offers a 15% discount to nonprofit organizations**, making it an affordable option for mission-critical communication needs. [Contact them here](https://www.onpage.com/contact-us/) and be sure to let them know you're with a nonprofit.
