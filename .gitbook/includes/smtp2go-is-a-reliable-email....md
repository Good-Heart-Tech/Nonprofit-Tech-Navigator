### SMTP2Go&#x20;

<div align="left"><figure><img src="../assets/smtp2go-logo.svg" alt="" width="150"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

**SMTP2GO** is a reliable email delivery service ideal for nonprofits needing to send authenticated email from websites, scanners, and line-of-business apps. With increasing email security requirements (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), SMTP2GO makes it easy to set up and verify your domain with DNS records to ensure emails reach inboxes.

* **Free Tier:** Send up to **1000 emails/month** at no cost.
* **Nonprofit Discount:** **30% off** the Starter Plan (normally $15/month) for higher volumes.

**Common Uses:** Website forms, Copiers/scanners, Donor CRMs and custom apps

**Why It Matters:** Authenticated email improves deliverability, protects your domain reputation, and ensures critical communications like receipts and alerts are delivered reliably.
