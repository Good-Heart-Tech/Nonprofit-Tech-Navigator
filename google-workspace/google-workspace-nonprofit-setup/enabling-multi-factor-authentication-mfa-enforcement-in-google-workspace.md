# Enabling Multi-factor Authentication (MFA) Enforcement in Google Workspace

1. **ENSURE MFA IS SET UP ON YOUR ADMIN ACCOUNT**.   This is extremely important to ensure you don't lock everyone out of their accounts. You can configure MFA for your account using this link: [https://myaccount.google.com/security?hl=en](https://myaccount.google.com/security?hl=en)
   * ![](<../../.gitbook/assets/image (38).png>)
2. Enforce MFA in this menu: [https://admin.google.com/ac/security/2sv?journey=32](https://admin.google.com/ac/security/2sv?journey=32)
   *   Use the settings shown in the screenshot below:&#x20;

       <figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (58).png" alt="" width="364"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
3. After enabling, all users will be prompted to configure MFA, and will not be able to access Google Workspace services unless they are enrolled. &#x20;
