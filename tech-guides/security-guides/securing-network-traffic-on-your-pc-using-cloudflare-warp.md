# Securing Network Traffic on Your PC Using Cloudflare WARP

Securing network traffic is crucial for nonprofits dealing with sensitive information. This guide provides a technical walkthrough on installing Cloudflare WARP, offering a free solution to enhance online security, a vital aspect for nonprofits safeguarding their digital assets.

### **Step 1: Download and Install WARP**

Visit [https://1.1.1.1](https://1.1.1.1/), and choose your platform. Download and run the installer, following on-screen instructions to complete the installation.

### **Step 2: Connect to WARP**

Launch the WARP client on your device, and connect to the service. No need to sign in—WARP is ready to secure your network traffic immediately.

### **Step 3: Verify Secure Connection**

Check the WARP client interface for a "**Connected**" status. Congratulations! Your network traffic is now encrypted and optimized for enhanced security.
